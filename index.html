Hangar Services

Ideally situated at the heart of the Central-Eastern European region, our hangar has the infrastructure needed to accommodate all of your operational requirements, ranging from: clean and secured parking place, storage for your spares & tools and facilities dedicated to your crew, passengers and clients (wifi, room, desk, bathroom). We can accommodate anything from light helicopters to Boeing 737-400.

Spare parts & tools sales and dispatch

Situated inside one of Europe’s leading aircargo hub, our facility offers one of the most complete stock of AN26 spare parts in Europe, as well as a wide selection of other Eastern and Western aircrafts spares and avionics ready for immediate dispatch. We also lease tools and equipment for specialist works and repairs.

Crew handling & workspace

Whether you need to accommodate VIPs, organize an aircraft presentation for clients or find a suitable centralized space to arrange a maintenance, we stand ready to help you with fully equipped workspace, bathrooms, storage for your tools and equipment, as well as a fully equipped apartment ready to welcome your crew. We can also arrange for vehicle transportation and a wide variety of other hospitality services upon request.
